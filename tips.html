On this page...

we're blessing you with a list of tips we've picked up along the way!

  • Model poses should look natural and so help them loosen up beforehand!
  • Scout your environment before beginning a shoot ALWAYS!!
  • Stay safe and be aware of your surroundings when taking photos
  • Don't stick to one angle and keep on changing angles while shooting!
  • Make sure your model is comfortable and respect their boundaries
  • Make use of natural light to your advantage
  • Spam the camera button to ensure you have plenty of options to pick when advancing to the editing stage
  • Always try to enhance your subject's key features
  • Don't be afraid to do uncomfortable or weird poses to capture the perfect shot!
  • Play around with editing features! You never know what can work!
  • You don't need the fancy stuff, you only need a camera
  • Look at others for inspiration!
