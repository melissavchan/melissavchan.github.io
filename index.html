Nice to see you!

We’re Star Studios and welcome to our guide of amateur photography! We’ll be teaching you the basics of how to make your photos appear amazing (even if you don’t have the fancy stuff). Our goal is to prove you can create polished photos at home and on a budget. This guide can range from selfies to amateur projects. We target images of people and portraits in general. Digesting large amounts of content can be hard. So we've decided to put together all our knowledge into a small website for you to understand. You don't need a ton of prior knowledge about photography to comprehend this guide. You only need a keen interest in taking cool photos!

Overview 

This simple, concise tutorial shows you portrait photography basics from start to finish. It will tackle angles, lighting and editing processes. As a gift, we have a page that gives extra tips and advice from personal experiences. Our studio focuses on capturing model portraits that make each person shine. So, if you're looking for a how-to about portraiture, this is the right place for you!

Requirements 

To start off, you need at least:

  • a device that can take photos
  • access to an editing software if available

Recommendations of devices are:

  • Mobile Phone
  • Digital cameras
  • Optional: Professional cameras

Editing Softwares:

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Picsart
  • DazzCam

We will be referencing Adobe Photoshop and DazzCam presets. Our photos will also be sourced from the iPhone 14 camera.
